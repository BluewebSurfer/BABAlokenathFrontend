About Us

We can proudly confide that we are the best Interior Designer in Guwahati because we have years of experiences in the same field. Our ultimate achievement would be turning the ideas into art and to create the most beautiful and functional environment. We offer a range of designs from home decors, furniture design, project management to false ceiling and display of the walls. Our projects are uniquely designed and we are always thinking out of the box.

The world is more hectic by the minute, but the place called Home where all your stress gets alleviated and we believe everyone deserves a beautiful and peaceful space. So keeping this in our mind, Baba lokenath Interior is here to bring your ideas into the reality of how your personal space should feel. We strive to create thoughtful interiors that delight and inspire you on a daily basis and we value function, comfort and beauty through classics design practices and artistic approach to composition.
